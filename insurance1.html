Indian government insurance

Agriculture insurance company of India Limited (AIC)

(AIC) is an Indian Central Public Sector undertaking the ownership of Ministry of Finance, Government of India. It offers yield-based and weather-based crop insurance programs in almost 500 districts of India.It covers almost 20 million farmers, making it the biggest crop insurer in the world in number of farmers served.
